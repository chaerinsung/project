j!nx

My teammate, Jen, and I decided to participate in the 2023 UCI Designathon as our first UI/UX experience. We were given 24 hours to design an app or website.

The prompt was: community and inclusivity

introduction.
problem.

Communities outside of your own do not intersect or interact, or it is difficult to do so.

j!nx is an app designed for students to define their hobbies or interests and connect with people with similar interests.

play on words (jinx) to find people with similarities as you that you would never know.

Why j!nx?

j!nx makes it easier for university students to meet people outside of their immediate or extended circles/communities.

j!nx makes it easier to find people with the same interests and niches.

solution & product proposal.
design process.
